## ANIMATION SPECIFICATIONS

### Global Effects
- **Page load:** Staggered fade-in from top
- **Scroll:** Parallax on background elements (0.5x speed)
- **Smooth scroll:** CSS scroll-behavior: smooth

### Hero
- **Background gradient:** 20s infinite loop, slow shift
- **Particles:** 50-100 particles, float upward, subtle
- **Text:** Fade in + slight translateY
- **CTA:** Subtle pulse glow

### Cards
- **Hover:** Lift 8px, shadow increases, border glows
- **Transition:** 0.3s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)

### Buttons
- **Hover:** Scale 1.02, background lightens
- **Active:** Scale 0.98
- **Focus:** Ring outline (accessibility)

### Stats/Numbers
- **Count up:** Animate from 0 when scrolled into view
- **Duration:** 2s
- **Easing:** ease-out
